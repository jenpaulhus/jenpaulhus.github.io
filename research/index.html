Current Projects

  • (with undergraduates) studying the alternating group as a Hurwitz group and Jacobian variety decompositions
  • (with the Geometry at the Frontier research group) studying questions about topological and conformal equivalence of group actions on Riemann surfaces
  • (with Aaron Wootton) studying actions of the alternating group on Riemann surfaces
  • (with various collaborators) creating a database on groups in the LMFDB, and improving a database of group actions on Riemann surfaces:
    • identifying topologically equivalent generating vectors
    • adding passports for when the quotient genus is not 0
    • higher genus data
    • quotient actions
