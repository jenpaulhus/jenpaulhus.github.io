Grading
  • Homework: Homework assignments will be due at the beginning of class just about every MWF. Assignments will be listed below. No late homework will be accepted but the lowest three homework scores will be dropped. Homework is 20% of your grade.
  • Exams: There will be three in-class exams on September 14, October 12, and November 16. Your lowest score will be 10% of your final grade and the other two exams will be 20% each.
  • Final: There will be a final which will count for 25% of your grade. The final exam for section 03 is 9:00 AM-12:00 PM on Wednesday, December 14. The final exam for section 05 is 2:00-5:00 PM on Thrusday, December 15 . Do not make plans to go home early.
  • Concept/Definition Sheet: Before every class you should spend about 15 minutes reading the material for that day and writing down definitions or brief explanations of the words listed below. Occasionally I will collect these lists and grade them for completeness. This score will be 5% of your final grade.

    Getting Help
